ABOUT US

Dr. Shruti Shah:

Pioneering Excellence in
Pain Management

-

Dr. Shruti Shah is a highly qualified Board Certified Anesthesiologist and Interventional Pain Management specialist with over 15 years of experience. She completed her residency in Anesthesiology and fellowship in Pain Management at Rutgers-Robert Wood Johnson Medical School in New Brunswick, New Jersey. Throughout her practice, Dr. Shah has treated numerous conditions, including acute and chronic neck pain, low back pain, headaches, neuropathy, arthritis pain, spinal stenosis, herniated discs, and sports injuries. Her approach to patient care emphasizes improving the quality of life by employing a combination of traditional and alternative methods.

Dr. Shah is proficient in various Interventional Pain Procedures, including Epidural steroid injections, Facet Injections, radio-frequency ablations, spinal cord stimulators,Peripheral Nerve Stimulators, Platelet-Rich Plasma (Regenerative Medicine) injections (regenerative medicine), joint injections, and Aesthetic Medicine.

Her expertise is recognized and acknowledged, as she holds dual certifications in Anesthesiology and Pain Medicine through the American Board of Anesthesiology.

Mission Statement

We are the specialists devoted to the art of pain management: diagnosing, managing,and treating acute pain, chronic pain conditions regenerative and aesthetic medicine.

We also aim to go beyond treatment of pain, reinforce your own healing powers and improve your quality of life and productivity and make you feel younger and healthier.
