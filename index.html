At Liberty Pain Care, we understand the impact that pain has on your life. We are committed to helping our patients locate the source of their pain, reduce it, and regain their mobility. Some pains that we treat include back pain, neck pain, and sciatica pain. Our ultimate goal is to help patients reclaim control and quality of their life.

Our care is tailored to the needs of each individual and their specific problems. Using a combination of Interventional Pain procedures and other treatment techniques like Physical Therapy, Prolotherapy and Regenerative Medicine, patients are able to manage pain and get back to their life.
